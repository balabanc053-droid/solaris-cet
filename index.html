FAQ

Frequently asked questions about SOLARIS CET, R2A Protocol, and tokenomics.

SOLARIS CET is a hyper-scarce utility token (9,000 fixed supply) on TON Blockchain that powers the Reason-to-Act (R2A) Protocol — a decentralized incentive framework for AI agent development and community growth.

Connect a TON-compatible wallet (Tonkeeper, MyTonWallet), then trade on DeDust: DeDust Pool. Always verify the contract address.

R2A (Reason-to-Act) is a reward mechanism where developers stake CET, submit AI tasks, receive on-chain validation, and earn proportional rewards based on contribution quality and impact.

We cannot provide financial advice. CET is a utility token with risks including market volatility, technology risk, and regulatory uncertainty. Only invest what you can afford to lose. Do your own research (DYOR).

Join our Discord or Telegram, participate in governance votes, submit feature requests, contribute code via GitHub, or help with community building. All contributions are valued.

We have submitted a listing application to CoinMarketCap. Approval timelines vary (typically 2-8 weeks). Follow our social channels for updates.
